All-Day Expander

Google Calendar caps the height of the all-day events strip at the top of the day/week view. When you have many all-day items, the extra ones get hidden behind the hourly grid below. This expands that strip so you can see them all.

Each button is a toggle — click it again to turn it off, and only one can be on at a time. Your choice is remembered and re-applied automatically the next time the calendar loads.
